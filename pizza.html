Our Family Story

Tony's Pizza Palace was founded in 1995 by Tony Marino, an Italian immigrant who brought authentic recipes from his hometown in Naples. What started as a small takeout operation has grown into a beloved community gathering place.

Today, Tony's sons Mario and Luigi continue the tradition, using the same family recipes and commitment to quality that made our pizza famous. Every pizza is hand-tossed, every sauce is homemade, and every customer is treated like family.
