Our Story

Brew Haven was born from a passion for great coffee and community. Founded in 2018 by Sarah and Mike Johnson, we've been serving our neighborhood with premium coffee, fresh pastries, and a warm atmosphere that feels like home.

Every morning, we source the finest beans from local roasters and bake our pastries fresh daily. Whether you're starting your day with a strong espresso or meeting friends for brunch, we're here to make your experience memorable.
